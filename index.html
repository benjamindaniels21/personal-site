About Me

I’m a composer turned software developer. When I’m not coding, I enjoy spending time with my pug, Bill, and building furniture. I became interested in coding after taking a course in generative media during my time in graduate school. The students were encouraged to work in different technologies and I was exposed to a bit of software called Max/MSP, which allows a user to physically connect different little tools with virtual cables, like a sort of mad scientist. The feeling of accomplishment after toiling for hours to get something to work and to display what I wanted on a screen (a generative poem, for example), was something novel to me at the time and I started to think about what a career in software might mean to me...[more]
